This comprehensive dataset contains daily stock market data for Toyota Motor Corporation (NYSE: TM), one of the world's largest automobile manufacturers. It includes complete Open, High, Low, Close (OHLC) price data and trading volume for over 40 years of trading history, making it ideal for financial analysis, time series forecasting, technical analysis, and machine learning applications.

Key Features
✅ Complete OHLC Data: Open, High, Low, Close prices for each trading day
✅ Trading Volume: Daily trading volume in shares
✅ Long-term Coverage: Over 40 years of historical data
✅ Clean and Consistent: Pre-processed for immediate analysis
✅ Time Series Ready: Properly formatted dates and chronological ordering
✅ Beginner Friendly: Simple CSV format with clear column names
